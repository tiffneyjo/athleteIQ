Athelete IQ

Athlete IQ equips high-potential young athletes with accurate, sport-specific training knowledge, so preventable preparation gaps don’t turn into injuries that cut athletic careers short.

Athlete IQ's Mission

We envision a youth sports system where talented athletes are not held back by preventable injuries, where training decisions are informed and proactive, and where access to evidence-based knowledge is not limited by resources, geography, or misinformation.

Long-term, we aim to build a standard for injury-aware training education that supports athletes, teams, and future coaches.
