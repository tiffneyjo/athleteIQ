Prevention Tracker

What is the Prevention Tracker?

The Prevention Tracker is a tool designed to help athletes build more consistent injury-prevention habits. It brings together key areas of physical preparation, such as mobility, stability, recovery, and workload awareness, into one place. Rather than reacting only after pain or injury shows up, the tracker encourages athletes to take a more proactive approach to their health and performance.

Who is it for?

The Prevention Tracker is for athletes who want to better understand their bodies and lower their risk of avoidable injury. It can be useful for athletes at different ages, levels, and stages of training, whether they are in-season, off-season, or returning from a setback. It is especially helpful for athletes who want a simple way to stay accountable to the small habits that support long-term performance.

What can it help with?

The tracker helps users identify areas they may need to pay more attention to, such as recovery, movement quality, or training balance. It is meant to support consistency by giving athletes a clearer picture of what they are doing well and what they may be neglecting. Over time, this can help users build routines that support both performance and injury prevention.

How does it work?

Users enter information related to their sport, training habits, and physical needs. Based on that information, the tracker highlights relevant prevention priorities and helps organize them in a structured way. The goal is to make prevention feel more manageable, personalized, and easy to track over time.
