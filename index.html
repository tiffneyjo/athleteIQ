Athlete IQ

Athlete IQ equips high-potential young athletes with accurate, sport-specific training knowledge, so preventable preparation gaps don’t turn into injuries that cut athletic careers short.

Our Mission

Athlete IQ's Mission

We envision a youth sports system where talented athletes are not held back by preventable injuries, where training decisions are informed and proactive, and where access to evidence-based knowledge is not limited by resources, geography, or misinformation.

Long-term, we aim to build a standard for injury-aware training education that supports athletes, teams, and future coaches.

Organizational Values

  1. Access to Knowledge
    Athletic potential should not be limited by a lack of education. We believe all athletes deserve access to accurate, understandable information about how to prepare for their sport safely.
  2. Prevention Over Reaction
    We prioritize preseason readiness, risk awareness, and early education to reduce injuries before they occur, rather than reacting after damage is done.
  3. Sport-Specific Understanding
    Different sports create different injury risks. Training and preparation should reflect the unique demands of each sport.
  4. Evidence-Informed, Age-Appropriate Education
    All Athlete IQ content is designed to be appropriate for youth athletes and grounded in established sports science principles, without medical diagnosis or treatment.
  5. Long-Term Athlete Development
    We value sustainable progress and long athletic careers over short-term performance or rushed returns from injury.
  6. Collaboration, Not Replacement
    Athlete IQ complements coaches, physiotherapists, athletic therapists, and medical professionals by improving athlete awareness and communication, not replacing professional care.
  7. Responsibility & Integrity
    We are transparent about our role as an educational organization and operate with care in all youth sport environments.
